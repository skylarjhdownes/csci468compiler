Life Cycle

Our team's development cycle followed something which generally resembled agile principles. Parts of the project were helpful to do as a group, however, parts of the assignment were better to do on an individual basis. The initial skeleton of the Parser was beneficial to split into four parts and have each group member handle one part. Once we got the Parser outlined, we took on tasks such as the Symbol Table and Semantic Analyzer.

Our group met weekly to discuss our direction and issues with the code, then split to work individually. We communicated heavily through email and github otherwise, and kept each other up to date on what we were working on.
